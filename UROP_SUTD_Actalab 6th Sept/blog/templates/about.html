About ChalcoSite.Online

As the name suggests, ChalcoSite.online is a data collection of Chalcogenide materials' properties. The site is run by ActaLab@SUTD and the data that is publicly available has been collected by researchers from the ActaLab group during their investigative processes.

Our aim is develop a robust and sustainable digital database of the properties that a vast array of Chalcogneide materials possess. Eventually, we hope to run machine learning programs on the large database to accurately predict new material properties and build a system of Artifical Intelligence that brings more efficiency and innovation into the research process of Material Scientists.

The Digital Aspects that Drive AI-based Research

COLLECTIVE KNOWLEDGE

Developing an impressive, comprehensive digital collection of knowledge so that any relevant information is never lost, only stored and shared.

INTERNET OF THINGS

Automation of data uploading through a wireless data flow network. Files from instruments are automatically transferred into an external server, where they are processed for data values to be entered automatically into the database.

BIG DATA ANALYTICS

Creating a structured database to hold the large volumes of seemingly unconncected data points for better scalability & more efficient queries. The organisation also aids data analytics to reveal patterns and connections between the materials and their properties.
